Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any experience to enroll in the beauty academy?

No experience is required! Our courses are designed for beginners and professionals alike. We provide hands-on training to enure you gain the skills and confidence to succeed in the beauty industry.

How do I book an appointment for nails, makeup, or lashes?

Booking is easy! You can call or Whatsapp on 068 357 5801 or visit us at Voortrekker Road, Bellville Central to schedule your appointment. Walk-ins are welcome, but bookings are recommended for guaranteed availability.

What beauty services do you offer?

We offer a range of services, including manicures, acrylic and gel nails, lash, extensions, makeup application, and more.Whether you need a quick touch-up or a full glam transformation, we've got you covered!
